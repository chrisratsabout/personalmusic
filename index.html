Personal Music


YouTube



Chris Ratsabout and Derek Baker are the two main collaborators in DEL AURA. The initial songwriting sessions lasted for a couple months after they met in 2014. The duo reconvened in the summer of 2020 and started releasing their music under this name.

DEL AURA is a creative outlet for them to experiment, explore other genres, grow as musicians, and improve their production skills.

Listen to DEL AURA’s music on Bandcamp, Spotify, Apple Music, and other streaming services.

Check out Derek’s solo project Lasidas on Bandcamp.
